About the Woods

A Forest Within a School

Stretching across the eastern edge of the Lawrenceville campus, the school woods are more than a backdrop — they are a living classroom, a sanctuary, and an ecosystem in their own right.

From Bowl Woods to the Meadow, each named section carries its own character: dense canopy, open meadow, creek-side habitat, and forested adventure. Together they form one of the most distinctive features of the Lawrenceville landscape.
